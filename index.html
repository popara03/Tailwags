Who are we?

We're AdoptMe, a non-profit organization located in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. In 2018, a group of volunteers founded our organization so we could come together to find homes for animals in need. Since then, we have found homes for over 4000 pets. Our mission is to protect animals and to find loving homes for each one of them.

Want to put a pet for adoption?

If for some reason you are not able to take care of your pet anymore, don't hesitate to contact us via our contact form! Send us photos of the pet you want to put up for an adoption and as well as information on your pet's health status, information you think is relevant and your contact. We will be happy to find a loving home for your pet!
